What to Wear

Prepare for a day filled with love and celebration by choosing an outfit that suits the occasion. Here's a guide to help you dress for the festivities:

  • Attire: Semi-formal attire is recommended for our wedding celebration. Please refrain from wearing jeans.
  • Color Palette: Kindly opt for attire featuring shades or elements of BLUE, as we aim for a cohesive theme, and we suggest avoiding white to let the bride shine.
  • Dress Code: While there's no strict dress code, we encourage you to embrace your personal style and dress in a way that makes you feel fantastic.

If you have any questions or need further clarification on the dress code, feel free to reach out. Your presence is what matters most, and we're excited to celebrate this joyous occasion with you!
